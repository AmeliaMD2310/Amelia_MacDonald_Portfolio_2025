About Me

I'm currently a second-year IT programming student at NSCC. I am eager to learn new programming languages and develop my skills through hands-on projects. I enjoy problem-solving and working with code to create solutions that can have a real-world impact. Throughout the last 2 years, I've worked on various projects involving Java, web development, mobile app development, C++, react and more.

During my time in the program, I've had the opportunity to explore different areas of software development — from building interactive websites to developing small mobile applications and creating logic-driven programs in Java and C++. Each project has helped me strengthen my understanding of core programming concepts, improve my debugging strategies, and gain confidence in translating ideas into functional applications.

I’m especially motivated by work that challenges me to think creatively and break down problems into manageable steps. Whether I’m designing a feature, optimizing code, or learning a new framework, I enjoy the process of figuring out how all the pieces fit together. As I move forward in my studies and career, I hope to continue expanding my technical skill set, collaborate on meaningful projects, and contribute to solutions that make a positive difference for users.

Outside of coding, I love spending time with my daughter, exploring nature, going for a nice scenic drive with some good music and indulging in a good book or movie. These activities help me recharge and bring fresh perspectives to my work.
